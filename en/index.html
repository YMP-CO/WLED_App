About the Project

Hello everyone! I'm 15 years old and I decided to create my own unusual project, specifically - WLED App

WLED App is an application that allows you to control LED strips using WLED. It provides extensive functionality for lighting setup, including color selection, lighting modes, and effects.

✓

Easy connection: Simple setup of WLED devices for instant control.

✓

Color customization: Choose color palettes directly in the app.

✓

Effect selection: Ability to choose effects for your WLED LED strip.

WLED App is the perfect tool for creating stylish and personalized lighting.
